About the Film

Title: Markova: Comfort Gay

Year: 2000

Director: Gil Portes

Synopsis: Based on the life of Walterina Markova, the film portrays his survival as a male sex slave during WWII and Japanese occupation.

Genre: Drama, Historical

Background: A poignant look at marginalized communities and historical events in the Philippines.
